Our Story

About The Gilded Perch

A premium aviary born from a deep love of avian life — and a belief that every bird deserves an exceptional start

Who We Are

More Than a Breeder. A Lifelong Partnership.

The Gilded Perch was founded on a simple but powerful conviction: the relationship between a parrot and its family is one of the most profound bonds in the natural world — and it deserves to begin with integrity, expertise, and genuine care.

We are a collective of certified aviculturists, avian veterinarians, and lifelong bird enthusiasts who have dedicated our careers to the responsible breeding and placement of the world's most remarkable parrot species. Each bird in our aviary is hand-raised from hatch, socialized with extraordinary care, and paired with the right family — not simply the first interested buyer.

We do not operate like a pet shop. We operate like the luxury aviary we are: selective, deliberate, and utterly committed to the welfare of every bird and every family we serve.

What We Stand For

Our Core Values

I

Health First, Always

Every bird in our aviary undergoes a comprehensive veterinary examination by a certified avian vet before being offered for sale. Our health guarantee covers every chick we place, and our post-sale support ensures you're never left to navigate health questions alone.

II

Ethical Breeding Practices

We breed exclusively in natural pair bonding situations, never using force-pairing or intensive production methods. Our breeding pairs have spacious, enriched enclosures and are monitored daily by experienced aviculturists. Bird welfare is non-negotiable.

III

Personal Buyer Support

When you purchase through The Gilded Perch, you gain access to your bird's personal breeder — not a call centre. We offer lifetime consultation services, diet and enrichment guidance, and are always available when you need expert advice about your companion.

IV

Full Legal Transparency

Every bird sold through The Gilded Perch comes with complete CITES documentation where legally required, full veterinary records from hatch, microchipping where applicable, and clear guidance on import requirements for your country.

V

Cognitive Enrichment

Parrots are among the most cognitively complex animals on earth. From day one, our chicks are raised with structured enrichment programmes — foraging challenges, socialization routines, and positive reinforcement training — that set them up for a life of mental flourishing.

VI

The Right Match, Every Time

Not every parrot suits every family. We take time to understand your lifestyle, experience level, and expectations before recommending a bird. Our goal is a harmonious lifelong relationship — and we won't compromise on that by rushing a placement.

How It Works

The Gilded Perch Process

We've designed every step to protect both you and the bird.

  1. 01

    Browse & Express Interest

    Explore our full collection of available parrots and submit a reservation request for any bird that captures your heart. No payment is required at this stage.

  2. 02

    Breeder Consultation

    Within 24 hours, your bird's personal breeder will contact you by email. This consultation covers the bird's history, personality, care needs, and answers all your questions.

  3. 03

    Documentation & Deposit

    Once both parties are happy to proceed, we prepare all required health certificates, CITES permits, and veterinary documentation. A deposit secures your bird while logistics are arranged.

  4. 04

    Delivery & Welcome

    Your parrot is transported with certified courier services, climate-controlled where necessary, with regular welfare checks throughout the journey. We guide you through every step of the arrival and settling-in process.

  5. 05

    Lifetime Support

    Your relationship with The Gilded Perch doesn't end at delivery. We provide ongoing dietary, behavioral, and veterinary guidance for the lifetime of your bird. You are never alone.
